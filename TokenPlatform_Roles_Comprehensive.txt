## üîÑ Role Interactions & Workflows

### **KYC Verification Flow:**
1. **Investor** submits KYC request
2. **Trusted Issuer** reviews and approves claims
3. **Admin** oversees the process
4. **Issuer** verifies KYC before token access

### **Token Investment Flow:**
1. **Investor** expresses interest in token
2. **Issuer** reviews and verifies KYC
3. **Issuer** approves purchase request
4. **Issuer** mints or transfers tokens
5. **Investor** can now trade and transfer tokens

### **Compliance Enforcement:**
1. **Issuer** sets token compliance rules
2. **Trusted Issuer** verifies investor eligibility
3. **Platform** enforces on-chain compliance
4. **Admin** monitors overall compliance

---

This role-based system ensures proper separation of concerns, regulatory compliance, and secure token management while maintaining the integrity of the T-REX security token standard.
